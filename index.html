About Me

I am a senior undergraduate student in the Electrical and Electronics Engineering Department at Boğaziçi Üniversitesi, İstanbul, Türkiye. My expected graduation date is January 2023. I was an Erasmus student at Politechnika Poznańska (PUT), Poznań, Poland, in Spring 2021.

I was with Antennas and Propagation Research Laboratory (BOUNtenna), İstanbul, Türkiye, as an Undergraduate Research Assistant from July 2019 to March 2022. Also, I did two internships at Turkcell and ASELSAN during the summers of 2020 and 2021, respectively.

I am currently with Poilabs, as an embedded software developer intern. My current research interests include communication systems and embedded software development.
